Explore Outdoors

Explore Outdoors is an app created to help you seemlessly plan your next trip to any national park in the US. It is linked to the 400+ national parks with a user-friendly interface. This app provides you with a location map, images and weather information for any park you wish to explore all in one place.




Try It Yourself


How does it work?


Step 1: Click on the start Button



Step 2: Select a state (required), then select any combination of activites and themes



Step 3: Click on any one of the park results



Step 4: view more details on the selected park



Tools: JavaScript, JQuery, HTML, Pure CSS, National Parks API, OpenWeatherMap API, Leaflet.js
